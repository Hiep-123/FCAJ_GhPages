During my internship on AWS, I had the opportunity to work on a project focused on building a serverless e-commerce platform on AWS. Through this experience, I learned how to apply academic knowledge in practice through a project-based learning experience, rather than participating in a real corporate project.

I also improved my ability to learn new tools quickly, work with technical documentation, and cooperate with teammates to solve problems effectively. The internship helped me strengthen both my technical foundation and my professional attitude.

To reflect on my performance objectively, I assessed myself using the following criteria:

| No. | Criteria | Self-assessment | Notes |
| --- | -------- | --------------- | ----- |
| 1 | Professional knowledge and skills | Good | I gained practical understanding of AWS services, CDK, serverless architecture, and deployment processes. |
| 2 | Ability to learn | Good | I adapted quickly to new tools and concepts during the project. |
| 3 | Proactiveness | Good | I was willing to explore tasks independently and ask for guidance when needed. |
| 4 | Sense of responsibility | Good | I completed assigned tasks carefully and tried to maintain quality in my work. |
| 5 | Discipline | Fair | I need to improve time management and follow the work plan more strictly. |
| 6 | Progressive mindset | Good | I was open to feedback and tried to improve my work continuously. |
| 7 | Communication | Fair | I can communicate clearly, but I still need to improve my confidence in presenting ideas. |
| 8 | Teamwork | Good | I was able to cooperate with others and contribute to the team’s progress. |
| 9 | Professional conduct | Good | I tried to act respectfully and responsibly in a professional environment. |
| 10 | Problem-solving skills | Fair | I can solve basic problems, but I still need more experience with complex technical issues. |
| 11 | Contribution to the project/team | Good | I contributed to the project through learning, testing, and supporting implementation tasks. |
| 12 | Overall | Good | The internship helped me grow in both technical and professional aspects. |

### Areas for improvement

- Improve discipline and time management in daily work.
- Strengthen communication skills, especially when presenting ideas or reporting progress.
- Continue developing problem-solving ability for more complex technical challenges.
